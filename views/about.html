Hobbies

Mountain Biking

I enjoy the outdoors quite a bit and try to spend as much time participating in outdoor activities as I can. Although I enjoy all sports, mountain biking is my favorite. Most of the time I am biking in Rhode Island, but I like to experience different areas of New England. Kingdom Trails in East Burke, Vermont is definitely my favorite so far.

Traveling

Traveling is one of my favorite hobbies, even though I cannot do it as much as I wish. I enjoy learning about different cultures and meeting new people. Throughout my life, I hope to be able to travel to as many countries and major landmarks as I can.

Technology

I have learned to appreciate everyday advances in technology. There is always something new to learn about. I am the kind of person that is always looking for something new. Once I find an idea, I dig deeper so I know how it works. My curiousity is one of my major assets.
